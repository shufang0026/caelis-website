Caelis Galería continued its participation in the West Bund Art & Design Fair, which was founded in 2014 and has become one of the leading international fairs for showcasing high-quality contemporary art. As the fair entered its second decade, Caelis Galería was honored to join forces with the West Bund Art & Design Fair to present a selection of contemporary surrealist and pop art works, contributing to and witnessing the positive and diverse development of the contemporary art ecosystem.

## Featured Artists

At this year's art fair, Caelis Galería presented works by nine artists from Spain, spanning different generations. The gallery featured new and innovative works by long-term representatives, such as contemporary Spanish surrealist painters **August Vilella** and **Cesc Abad**, as well as new oil paintings by the emerging "post-2000s" artist **Pedro Hoz**, whose work combining surrealism and classical aesthetics has already received acclaim in the industry.

## About West Bund Art & Design

West Bund Art & Design has established itself as one of Asia's most influential contemporary art fairs since its founding in 2014. Located in Shanghai's West Bund cultural corridor, the fair brings together leading galleries from China and internationally, creating a vital platform for contemporary art dialogue.
