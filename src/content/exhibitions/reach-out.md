Jaime Sancorlo's solo exhibition "Reach Out" at Caelis Galería presents the artist's unique vision through a body of work that combines meticulous painting technique with an exploration of imagery drawn from personal archives and historical references.

Sancorlo's practice is characterized by a sophisticated dialogue between image and meaning, drawing from old photographs and personal memory to create works that speak to universal themes of human experience. His works demonstrate a remarkable balance between technical precision and emotional resonance.

The exhibition invites viewers to engage with the artist's ongoing exploration of form, color, and narrative — each canvas serving as a window into a carefully constructed world of visual poetry.
