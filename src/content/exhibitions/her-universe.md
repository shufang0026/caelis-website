"Her Universe" is a group exhibition at Caelis Galería that brings together works exploring the multifaceted nature of feminine experience, identity, and creative expression.

The exhibition features artists whose practices engage with themes of personal and collective histories, emotional landscapes, and the ongoing dialogue between tradition and contemporary perspectives. Each work offers a unique perspective on what it means to inhabit, imagine, and transform the world.

Through diverse media and approaches, the exhibition invites viewers to encounter a rich tapestry of artistic voices — each contributing their own universe of meaning, memory, and imagination.
