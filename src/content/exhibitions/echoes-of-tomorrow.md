Zhang Huan powerful works, rooted in performance and bodily experience, meet Yayoi Kusama infinite universes of dots and mirrors. Their practices, though distinct in origin and expression, share a profound commitment to exploring the boundaries between self and world, individual and collective, past and future.

This exhibition creates unexpected resonances between two of the most influential artists of our time.
