Arol's solo exhibition "Pink Punk Mood" at Caelis Galería reveals the artist's fascinating journey from visual design and printmaking to computer painting, and finally to oil painting on wood — a medium through which he interprets his surrealistic artistic ideas.

The subtlety of design and the finesse of computers are now harmoniously blended in Arol's dreamy pink punk theater. His work invites viewers into a world where familiar forms and unexpected elements coexist in compositions that challenge conventional boundaries.

Arol's artistic practice explores the boundary between the digital and the physical, creating works that are both deeply personal and universally resonant — a testament to the artist's ongoing dialogue between technique, imagination, and emotional expression.
