The first chapter of the philosophical discussion on "Time and Existence" — a group exhibition at Caelis Galería bringing together the works of 16 Caelis artists. As Heraclitus wrote, no man ever steps in the same river twice. The exhibition invites viewers to contemplate the passage of time and the nature of existence through the diverse artistic voices of the gallery's represented artists.
