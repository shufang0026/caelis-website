Liu Wei monumental installations challenge our perception of architectural space, while Sarah Chen delicate paper works explore the ephemeral nature of form. Tomas Saraceno suspended sculptures create immersive environments that blur the line between artwork and atmosphere.

Together, these works invite visitors to consider how silence, both visual and conceptual, can speak as powerfully as material presence. The exhibition encourages a meditative engagement with space itself, asking us to see what exists in the spaces between.
