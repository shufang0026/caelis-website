Elang Sutajaya's solo exhibition "Lift the Spirit" at Caelis Galería presents a dynamic collection of works that reflect the Indonesian artist's distinctive approach to contemporary painting.

Sutajaya's work is marked by a vivid use of color and a keen observation of everyday life, translated through a personal visual language that bridges cultural traditions and contemporary expression. His paintings often draw from personal experience and observation, weaving together elements of daily life into compositions that are both intimate and expansive.

The exhibition offers a window into the artist's ongoing artistic journey — one that is deeply rooted in his cultural background while speaking to universal themes of hope, movement, and transformation.
